To install the chart:
 helm upgrade myapp ./charts/bluegreen-app -n myns --install --set
activeColor=blue
To update green images (example):
 helm upgrade myapp ./charts/bluegreen-app -n myns --install --reuse-values --
set app.imageWebGreen=docker.io/you/web:tag
To cut over:
 helm upgrade myapp ./charts/bluegreen-app -n myns --install --reuse-values --
set activeColor=green
